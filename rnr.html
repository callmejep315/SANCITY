REWARDS AND RECOGNITION

Project HIYAS

(Honoring Individuals and institutions Yielding Amazing Services)

The Gawad HIYAS, created in 2017, effectively tracks employees’ performance in their respective roles in the field. It is a Division-level Rewards and Recognition program applicable to teaching, teaching-related, and non-teaching employees of SDO Santiago City. This annual event recognizes outstanding accomplishments and innovations of employees that contribute to the effective and efficient delivery of education services. Its implementing rules serve as basis for achieving the Prime HRM Maturity Level II in 2021. Thus, adhering the principle of providing incentives and awards to the employees radiates exemplary performance and behavior among them.

As an organization, DepEd Santiago City sustains a culture of teamwork and excellence, with rewards and recognition as one of the pillars of the Division’s PRIME HRM. This commitment to recognizing and rewarding exemplary performance promotes a sense of collaboration and motivates employees to strive for excellence, resulting in the propelled delivery of basic educational services to its clienteles.

Through Gawad HIYAS, the Division ensures that its employees are recognized, motivated, and equipped to deliver quality education services. With this ongoing dedication to improvement and service, SDO Santiago City as certified ISO remains poised to its evolving needs and contribute to the advancement of education in the community.
