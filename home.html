WHAT IS SANCITY?

SANCITY, or the Simplified Access and Network to Centralized personnel Information-bank Truly Yielding, is an advanced personnel information management system designed to complement the PERSONIS framework by filling any data gaps and providing top management with a comprehensive, centralized dashboard. It seamlessly integrates with various databases, including Google Sheets, ensuring that all relevant personnel information is accessible and organized in one place. This enables quick, accurate, and efficient retrieval of critical data, enhancing decision-making processes at the highest levels of the organization.
