PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Project SMART

(Smart Management of Accomplishments and Rating with Technical assistance)

The Results-based Performance Management System (RPMS) of DepED supports the Vision, Mission, and Values of the Agency as it continuously endeavors to evolve as a learner-centered public institution.

Centered on the link between the individual performance with the organizationâ€™s vision, mission, and strategic goals, the Performance Management System is envisioned as a strategy, method, and tool to ensure that office and employee functions are fulfilled. Likewise, it is directed towards monitoring, assessing, and evaluating the accomplishments.

It is a mechanism that ensures that the employee achieves the objectives set by the organization for the specific positions, the organization, on the other hand, achieves the objectives that it has set itself in its strategic plan.

The PMS follows the four-stage cycle: performance planning and commitment, performance monitoring and coaching, performance review and evaluation, performance rewarding and development planning.
