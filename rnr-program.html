REWARDS AND RECOGNITION

Programs

HIYAS, being the over-all Awards System in the Division, also encourages schools and other offices to institutionalize their own reward systems. Awardees from these levels will then be entered into the cluster and Division level search. This qualifying standard contextualizes other categories of attaining the fruit of hardships and sacrifices as the employees of SDO Santiago City strive for excellence

MONETARY PROGRAMS

  1. GAWAD HIYAS-TANGLAW (Tangible Accomplishments, Notable Gains, and Laudable Acts of Workmanship) is the annual commendation and Type of Monetary Award given to an employee and institution with exceptional services that sustain improvement in the teaching-learning process and schoolâ€™s key performance indicators to wit;

    1. Most Outstanding Non-Teaching Level I
    2. Most Outstanding Non-Teaching Level II
    3. Most Outstanding Teacher (Elementary & Secondary)
    4. Most Outstanding Master Teacher (Elementary & Secondary)
    5. Most Outstanding Head Teacher (Elementary & Secondary)
    6. Most Outstanding Cluster ALS Coordinator
    7. Most Outstanding Mobile ALS Teacher
    8. Most Outstanding Teacher in Charge (Elementary & Secondary)
    9. Most Outstanding Principal (Elementary and Secondary)
    10. Most Outstanding Education Program Supervisor
    11. Most Outstanding Chief Education Supervisor
    12. Best Performing School

  2. GAWAD HIYAS-KINANG (Kawaning Idolo na Nagpapakita ng Abilidad para sa Natatanging Gawad) recognizes the quarterly performance of personnel in the SDO proper. The following awards are given to division personnel who excels in the different aspects to wit;

    1. Gawad Listo for most punctual employee
    2. Gawad Sipag for most industrious employee
    3. Gawad Modelo for employees who observe proper wearing of uniforms
    4. Gawad Kaarawan for employees who celebrate their natal day
    5. On the Spot Gawad Honesto, for employees whose act of honesty is evidently demonstrated with honest deeds, Gawad Commendable Service for employees who demonstrated exigency of service
    6. Gawad Kalikasan for offices that implement the program on 7s (Sort, Systematized, Shine, Standardized, Sustain, Safe and Spirit) in the division
    7. Gawad Maagap (Early Bird) for an employee/school that demonstrates most punctual in any of the activities conducted in the division.
    8. Customer Service Award for customers/clients who satisfactorily sustain the services provided by respective offices/sections by quarter.

  3. GAWAD HIYAS-KISLAP (Kindhearted Individuals with Superb Legacy and Astonishing Pledge to educational services) honors employees whose length of service reflects remarkable contribution in realizing the goal of the department to wit;

    1. Loyalty Award - type of Monetary Award for employees who have completed at least 10 years, and every five years thereafter, of continuous and satisfactory service in the government
    2. Retirement Award for employees who serve the department with utmost length of desirable and educational services. Monetary Award is given to retiree who are active member/s of the SCETERA during MANCOM by the Santiago City Employees,Teachers and Retirees Association(SCETRA).
