PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

This Framework shows the interconnection of the PMS to the three other System of PRIME HRM. The IPCRF/OPCRF as the main tool of Performance Rating of the personnel and office serves as reference and requirement for promotion, reclassification, professional opportunities, search for awards, and monetary awards. The Learning and Development provision however is embedded and provided progressively or formatively as it is evident in all phases of the PMS to ensure growth and improvement of the personnel in terms of the specific KRAs.

The RPMS is an HROD intervention that will help ensure the strategic, responsive, and effective delivery of Human Resources Management and Development (HRMD) services of all levels of DepEd so that it can effectively implement a learner-centered, school-based management system and the K to 12 strategies to improve the quality of education in public schools.

Profoundly committed to serve its learners and stakeholders, the Schools Division of Santiago City adheres to the Performance Management System Manual in conformance with the DepEd Order No. 2, s.2015.

Dubbed as PrEP or Priming Excellence in Performance, this Operations Manual introduces the performance management system of SDO Santiago City for its diverse human resources anchored on related policies of the CSC and of the DepED. Moreover, customized and contextualized policies, guidelines processes, systems, and tools were incorporated.
