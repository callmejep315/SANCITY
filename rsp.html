Project CORE

The Project CORE is one of the programs to meet the criteria of the Program to Institutionalize Meritocracy and Excellence in Human Resource Management (PRIME-HRM). The Project CORE is important to ensure that school personnel, teaching and nonteaching shall guarantee steps to equitably enjoy the opportunities among teaching and nonteaching organizational effectiveness. Important details of the different Means of Verifications to be evaluated during the assessment of documents shall be provided by these localized guidelines on selection, promotion and appointment of teaching, related teaching and nonteaching personnel of the department
